Analytics Dashboard for B+ Foundation

As part of JP Morgan’s CodeForGood challenge, our team got paired with the Andrew McDonough B+ Foundation to solve their problem of not getting enough information on the people who donated. In doing so, our team created an analytics platform that analyzed their data and linked it to those who are most likely to refer others to donate. We also updated their donation form by adding fields for additional information through a Google Chrome extension (we were not given access to their website code). Our team made it into the final four teams!

Data Analysis on Salary

For a data journalism class at UNC, I used data obtained through public records requests on faculty salaries in response to protests over lack of funding for the arts department. I merged several datasets to compare salaries across departments and shared visualizations via Tableau. Check out a draft of the story here.
